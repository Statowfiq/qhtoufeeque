      Hello all , 

      i am facing this error when i am trying to make ant clean all after i have created an extension of warehousingfacades 


      Duplicate definition of attribute in customwarehousingfacade , it has been already defined in warehousingfacades

      Steps i followed :- 

      1) make warehousingfacades as a template by adding 

      <meta key="extgen-template-extension" value="true"/> into extensioninfo.xml

      2) added extgen.properties with the following configuration 
      YEXTNAME_TOKEN=warehousingfacades
      YPACKAGE_TOKEN=warehousingfacadespackage
      YMANAGER_TOKEN=warehousingfacadesManager
      YCLASSPREFIX_TOKEN=warehousingfacades
      YGENERATED_TOKEN=Generated

      3)ant extgen then warehousingfacades then customwarehousingfacade 

      4) i added customwarehousingfacade  to localextensions.xml and removed warehousing facade from it

      5) ant clean all 

      i want to know the best practice for the beans duplication after creating the extension from another extension which is not a template and we made it a template?
